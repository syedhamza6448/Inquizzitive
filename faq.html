Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to the most common questions about Inquizzitive

How do I create my first quiz?

Creating your first quiz is easy! Simply click on "Create a Quiz" from the homepage, fill in your quiz details like title and description, add your questions with multiple choice answers, and click "Create Quiz" when you're done. You can customize themes and settings to make your quiz unique.

Is Inquizzitive free to use?

Yes! Inquizzitive offers a free tier that allows you to create unlimited quizzes, share them with others, and view basic analytics. We also offer premium features for users who need advanced functionality like detailed analytics, custom branding, and priority support.

How many questions can I add to a quiz?

There's no limit to the number of questions you can add to your quiz. However, we recommend keeping quizzes between 5-20 questions for the best user experience. Longer quizzes may lead to user fatigue and lower completion rates.

Can I customize the appearance of my quiz?

Inquizzitive offers extensive customization options. You can choose from pre-made themes or create your own custom theme by selecting your preferred colors for backgrounds, text, and accent elements. This helps match your quiz to your brand or personal style.

How do I share my quiz with others?

Once you've created your quiz, you'll receive a unique link that you can share via email, social media, or any other platform. You can also embed the quiz directly on your website using the provided embed code. The quiz is instantly accessible to anyone with the link.

Can I see who took my quiz and their results?

Yes! Inquizzitive provides detailed analytics for your quizzes. You can see completion rates, average scores, individual question performance, and anonymous user responses. For privacy reasons, we don't collect personal information unless users voluntarily provide it.

What types of questions can I create?

Currently, Inquizzitive supports multiple choice questions with up to 4 answer options. Each question can have one correct answer. We're working on adding more question types like true/false, fill-in-the-blank, and short answer questions in future updates.

Can I edit my quiz after publishing it?

Yes, you can edit your quiz at any time from your dashboard. You can modify questions, update answers, change settings, or adjust the theme. Changes are applied immediately, so anyone accessing the quiz link will see the updated version.

Is my data secure on Inquizzitive?

Security is our top priority. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data, secure servers with regular backups, and follow best practices for data protection. We never share your personal information with third parties without your consent.

Can I use Inquizzitive for educational purposes?

Inquizzitive is perfect for educational use. Teachers can create quizzes for their students, trainers can assess learning progress, and students can create study materials. We offer special features for educators including class management and detailed progress tracking.
