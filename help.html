Getting Started

Welcome to Inquizzitive! Here's everything you need to know to get started with creating and sharing quizzes.

Create Your Account

Sign up for a free account to start creating quizzes, track your progress, and access all features.

Create Your First Quiz

Use our intuitive quiz builder to create engaging quizzes with multiple choice questions, custom themes, and more.

Share Your Quiz

Share your quiz with friends, students, or colleagues using a simple link or embed it on your website.

Creating Quizzes

Learn how to create engaging and effective quizzes using our powerful quiz builder.

Quiz Settings

Configure your quiz title, description, difficulty level, category, and time limits to match your needs.

Adding Questions

Add multiple choice questions with up to 4 answer options. Mark the correct answer for each question.

Custom Themes

Personalize your quiz appearance with custom colors and themes to match your brand or style.

Managing Your Quizzes

Organize, edit, and track the performance of your quizzes from your dashboard.

View Analytics

Track quiz performance, view completion rates, and analyze user responses to improve your content.

Edit Existing Quizzes

Update questions, change settings, or modify themes of your existing quizzes at any time.

Delete Quizzes

Remove quizzes you no longer need from your dashboard to keep your workspace organized.

Troubleshooting

Common issues and their solutions to help you get the most out of Inquizzitive.

Connection Issues

If you're experiencing connectivity problems, check your internet connection and try refreshing the page.

Mobile Compatibility

Inquizzitive works on all devices. If you're having issues on mobile, try using the latest version of your browser.

Browser Issues

Clear your browser cache and cookies, or try using a different browser if you encounter any problems.

Can't find what you're looking for? Our support team is here to help you with any questions or issues.
